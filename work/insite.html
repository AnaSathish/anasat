INsite Researcher and Developer

October 2018 - Present

INsite Lab creates universally usable technologies and educate others with the knowlegde we have gained. I take pride in being a member of this lab as creating accessible technologies is one of my passions.

I work on the development team, which builds functional prototypes for research projects. Currently, we are developing a voice acitivated personal assitant (VAPA) that is more suitable for blind users as they prefer VAPAs that can change their speech rate, pitch, volume, etc. through voice commands.

  • Leading a team of 8 in modifying Mycroft, an open-source voice assistant for Linux OS, so that users can dynamically modify audio settings, such as speed, volume, pitch, and verbosity

  • Creating an Android companion app for Mycroft in which users can communicate with Mycroft and modify the audio settings with both voice-based commands and gesture recognition

  • Working with PhD students to understand how people with disabilities navigate public spaces with voice assistants

  • Detailing application requirements, and analyzing the functionality of the application to ensure a quality product

  • Writing thorough documentation for implemented features
