Alameda County Software Intern

June 2018 - August 2018

As an intern for Alameda County's IT Department, I was able to aid in the digital transformation of AC's websites migrating them from IBM WebSphere to Red Hat JBoss. I was able to learn more about web developement and the migration process as a whole. Check out my internship presentation here.

  • Migrated 10 Java applications from IBM WebSphere to Red Hat JBoss using Maven as a build automation tool to modernize Alameda County’s websites

  • Tested functionality and accessibility of Alameda County’s official government websites and committed projects to production

  • Collaborated in an agile environment with Senior Systems Analysts to ensure efficiency of products
